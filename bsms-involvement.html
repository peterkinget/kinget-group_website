BS/MS INVOLVEMENT

​UNDERGRADUATE & M.S. STUDENT RESEARCH INVOLVEMENT

  • Our group has an active program of undergraduate and M.S. student research involvement during the semester and summer and we also participate in the School's Undergraduate Reseach Involvement Program (URIP).

  • Self-driven, highly motivated candidates for projects for credit can contact Prof. Kinget. Before emailing, go carefully through this website. Please make sure your email includes details on your education (resume, grades, ...), on your motivation for doing a research project and on your prior research or design experience (copies of papers, reports, ...). Start the subject of your email with "[Student Interested in a Research Project]".

IEEE-MTT Fellowships

  • The IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society offers undergraduate fellowships for senior undergraduate students who want to do a research project. Details can be found from the society's homepage www.mtt.org -> Awards -> Undergraduate Scholarships. Contact Prof. Kinget if you are interested to apply.
